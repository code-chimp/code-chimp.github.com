About me

Hello, my name is Tim Goshinski and I live in Omaha, Nebraska with my lovely wife and amazing daughter. My days are spent developing web applications primarily in .NET and SQL Server, but long after my family has gone to bed I generally start tinkering on my Linux box, or one of my Windows machines … in a Linux VirtualBox. I have fun seeing what trouble I can get myself into with the new tech du jour currently generating buzz over at Proggit. Sometimes I chase stuff like Sass or jQuery for immediate benefit in my chosen profession — other times I try to broaden my horizons by playing with languages like F#, Racket, or Haskell just for the fun of it.

Code-chimp is my space to document personal projects that grab my interest. I write the posts mostly in a tutorial or "walk-through" format primarily so that months later I can remember what I've done, and if anyone else is interested maybe they can follow along as well. Since little of what I do is truly original I will endeavor to cite references to the original source material where appropriate.

Thanks for stopping by!
Tim
