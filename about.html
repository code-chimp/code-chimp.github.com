About me

Hello, my name is Tim Goshinski and I live in Omaha, Nebraska with my lovely wife and amazing daughter.

I work primarily with .NET web applications, but I fancy myself to be more of a polyglot than I most likely am. I'm not saying that I have been at this for a long time, but when I started out the term "pair programming" meant that I was tasked with writing our code in cuneiform on wet clay tablets while my partner warmed up the vacuum tubes in his armpits. Ah, heady days those.

Boomshanka!
